Simple Voice-to-Text

Click the microphone and start speaking. Your words will be converted to text instantly.

Press the microphone to start

How to use:

  • Click the microphone button to start voice recognition
  • Speak clearly into your microphone
  • Click again to stop (or it will stop automatically after a pause)
  • Use the copy button to copy the transcribed text
  • Works best in Chrome, Edge, and other Chromium-based browsers

100% free · No sign up required · Your data stays on your device
